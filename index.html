About the Company

iINTELLIGENCEi is a technology company based in Chennai, India, which focuses on web design and development, web applications development, android development, e-commerce, technology and online education. iINTELLIGENCEi is founded by Dr T Sasimurugan on August 09, 2019. The company started as on e-commerce print-on-demand business. The popular Chotees.com website sells t-shirts for men, women, kids and toddlers. It also sells sports-Cap and drinkware. Now, the Company has expanded to web design and development, web applications development, android development, technology and online education. iINTELLIGENCEi is established through technological innovation and passion for coding the web.
